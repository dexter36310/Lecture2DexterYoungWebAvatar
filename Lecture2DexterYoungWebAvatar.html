Nintendo Lecture Series Presents Dexter Young

Dexter Young wants to live in a world where he can sleep where ever he wants to and does not get a curfew so that his parents does not nag about him to go to sleep.
As a sleep technician, he has been featured on the Nobel Prize for his leading research on sleeping.

When he is not sleeping, you can find Dexter Young staying awake for 2 months.

His next project is Coco and hits the internet on December 14th, 2041.
